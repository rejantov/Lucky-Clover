Tokyo Revengers

Type: Spring 2021 Anime
Plot Summary: Takemichi Hanagaki's life is at an all-time low. Just when he
thought it couldn't get worse, he finds out that Hinata Tachibana, his ex-
girlfriend, was murdered by the Tokyo Manji Gang: a group of vicious criminals
that has been disturbing society's peace for quite some time. Wondering
where it all went wrong, Takemichi suddenly finds himself travelling through
time, ending up 12 years in the past—when he was still in a relationship with
Hinata. Realizing he has a chance to save her, Takemichi resolves to infiltrate
the Tokyo Manji Gang and climb the ranks in order to rewrite the future and
save Hinata from her tragic fate.

Genre: Action, Drama, School, Shounen
Released: 2021
Status: Ongoing
Other name: 東京リベンジャーズ
